About Me

Hello, and welcome to my homepage!
As a faculty member, my primary expertise lies in teaching Applied Mathematics courses. I enjoy developing applications and writing code to solve real-world problems. Recently, my work has focused on projects involving Machine Learning and AI-related topics. Some of my projects aim to improve education more broadly. These include designing effective study habits and developing innovative tools for delivering impactful courses.
